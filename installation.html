About PV

SOLAR PV(PhotoVoltaics)

What is Solar PV?

It is a technology that converts sunlight into electricity using solar panels. It is a sustainable, eco-friendly and cost-effective solution to harness renewable energy.

What are the benefits of installing rooftop solar PV plants?

  • 1. Saving on Electricity Bills.
  • 2. Generate Green Energy.
  • 3. No Maintenance
  • 4. Easy Monitoring
  • 5. Long Life

Other Benefits

  • 1. Value Creation for the Roof.
  • 2. Natural Cooling of the top floor due to reduced direct radiation.
